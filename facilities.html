Facilities

Cardio Section

Our gym offers a range of top-quality cardio equipment to support your fitness journey. From high-tech treadmills with adjustable speeds and heart rate monitors, to smooth, low-impact ellipticals and comfortable stationary bikes, we have options for all fitness levels. We also feature rowing machines for a full-body workout and stair climbers to boost lower-body strength. Each machine tracks key metrics like calories burned and heart rate, helping you monitor your progress. Our spacious cardio area is well-ventilated and regularly maintained for a clean, comfortable workout experience.

Strength Section

Our gym offers a comprehensive strength training facility designed to help you build muscle, improve endurance, and achieve your fitness goals. It features a wide range of equipment, including free weights, dumbbells, barbells, resistance machines, and power racks. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced lifter, you'll find the tools you need for effective workouts, including squat racks, bench presses, cable machines, and kettlebells. Our facility also provides space for functional training, allowing you to perform bodyweight exercises and circuit workouts. With knowledgeable staff available for guidance, you can confidently work on building strength and improving overall fitness.

Studio

Fitness studio is a dedicated space within the gym designed for specialized workouts. The studio is equipped with equipment specific to the type of class offered, such as mats, weights, or stationary bikes. The studio can accommodate 25-30 people, providing a more intimate and focused environment for personal instruction and group participation. The studio is designed with mirrors, sound systems, and proper ventilation to enhance the workout experience.

Boxing

Our boxing facility offers a fully equipped space for both beginners and experienced athletes. With professional-grade punching bags, speed bags, and a dedicated ring, it's perfect for improving technique, strength, and conditioning. The area is designed to provide a safe and motivating environment for solo training or group classes. Whether you're looking to learn the basics or sharpen your skills, our boxing facility caters to all levels with expert instruction and state-of-the-art equipment.

Rejuvenation

Our Rejuvenation Facility is designed to help you recover and unwind after a tough workout. Featuring a sauna to relax muscles and promote detoxification, along with an ice bath to reduce inflammation and accelerate recovery, our facility offers a perfect balance of heat and cold therapy. Whether you're looking to soothe tired muscles, improve circulation, or enhance overall well-being, our rejuvenation amenities provide the ultimate post-workout recovery experience.
